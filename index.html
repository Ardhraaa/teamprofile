About Us

We were formed at the Magic event, and honestly, the real magic wasn’t just the hackathon, it was how we came together, survived on zero sleep and too much Red Bull, and still managed to pull off something amazing.

What started as a random team-up quickly turned into a powerhouse of creativity and chaotic problem-solving. With members from different branches of engineering—electronics to computer science, business systems to biomedical—our conversations jump from circuits to software, from algorithms to ‘Wait, does this even work?’

Hardworking? Absolutely. Highly skilled? We’d like to think so. Running purely on Red Bull and last-minute determination? No doubt.
